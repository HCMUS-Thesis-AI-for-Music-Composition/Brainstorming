About The Key Of F Minor

Stardust is written in the key of F Minor. According to the Theorytab database, it is the 8th most popular key among Minor keys and the 19th most popular among all keys. Minor keys, along with major keys, are a common choice for popular music. The three most important chords, built off the 1st, 4th and 5th scale degrees are all minor chords (F minor, B♭ minor, and C minor).
